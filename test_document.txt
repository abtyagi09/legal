CONTRACT AGREEMENT

This Contract Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of February 4, 2026, by and between:

Party A: ABC Corporation, a Delaware corporation
Party B: XYZ Legal Services, LLC

WHEREAS, Party A desires to retain Party B for legal consulting services;
WHEREAS, Party B agrees to provide such services under the terms set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
   Party B shall provide legal consulting services as requested by Party A, including but not limited to contract review, compliance advisory, and legal research.

2. COMPENSATION
   Party A shall pay Party B at the rate of $300 per hour for services rendered.

3. TERM
   This Agreement shall commence on February 4, 2026, and continue for a period of one (1) year unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Agreement.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
   Both parties agree to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all proprietary and confidential information disclosed during the term of this Agreement.

5. TERMINATION
   Either party may terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days written notice.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first above written.

ABC Corporation                    XYZ Legal Services, LLC
By: John Smith                     By: Jane Doe
Title: CEO                         Title: Managing Partner
Date: February 4, 2026             Date: February 4, 2026
